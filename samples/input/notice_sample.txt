This law firm represents Hartwell Industries, Inc. ("Hartwell") in connection with the above-
referenced Agreement. We write to provide formal notice of default pursuant to Section 12.1
of the Agreement and to demand cure within the time period specified therein.

NOTICE OF BREACH

Hartwell has determined that Meridian Capital Group LLC is in material breach of the Agreement
for the following reasons:

    1.  UNAUTHORIZED THIRD-PARTY COMPENSATION. On or about August 15, 2023, Hartwell
        learned that Meridian accepted a payment of $750,000.00 from NorthStar Ventures
        LLC, a prospective investor solicited on Hartwell's behalf, without obtaining
        Hartwell's prior written consent as required under Section 7.2 of the Agreement.
        This constitutes a material breach of Meridian's fiduciary and contractual duties.

    2.  MISREPRESENTATION OF INVESTOR COMMITMENTS. Meridian, through its agent David
        Thornton, represented to Hartwell on multiple occasions between May 2023 and
        August 2023 that NorthStar Ventures and two additional institutional investors
        had provided verbal commitments to participate in Hartwell's Series B financing.
        Hartwell has now confirmed that no such commitments were ever made.

    3.  FAILURE TO PERFORM. Meridian has materially failed to fulfil its obligations
        under Section 3 of the Agreement to use commercially reasonable efforts to
        identify, solicit, and secure qualified investors for the Series B round.

DEMAND FOR CURE

Pursuant to Section 12.1 of the Agreement, Meridian is hereby directed to cure the above
breaches within fifteen (15) business days of receipt of this Notice, specifically by:

    (a) Providing full written disclosure of all compensation received from any party
        connected to Hartwell's proposed financing round, including but not limited to
        NorthStar Ventures LLC;

    (b) Immediately ceasing any further representations to Hartwell or third parties
        regarding the status of investor commitments; and

    (c) Returning to Hartwell the $750,000 improper payment or providing written
        confirmation of intention to return same within 30 days.

RESERVATION OF RIGHTS

Hartwell expressly reserves all rights and remedies available under the Agreement, at law,
and in equity, including the right to seek injunctive relief, specific performance,
compensatory damages, punitive damages, and any other relief to which Hartwell may be
entitled. This Notice shall not constitute a waiver of any claim or right.

If Meridian fails to cure the stated breaches within the time period specified herein,
Hartwell will have no choice but to pursue all available legal remedies without further
notice.

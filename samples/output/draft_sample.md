## Claims & Allegations

**Count I — Breach of Contract (against Meridian):**
Hartwell alleges Meridian materially breached the Master Services Agreement dated January 10, 2023 by: (a) accepting a $750,000 payment from NorthStar Ventures LLC in violation of Section 7.2's prohibition on unauthorized third-party compensation [³]; (b) failing to use commercially reasonable efforts to secure qualified investors [⁴]; and (c) failing to disclose material conflicts of interest [⁴].

**Count II — Fraud (against Meridian and Thornton):**
Between January 2023 and September 2023, Thornton allegedly made material misrepresentations to Hartwell that multiple institutional investors had committed to the Series B round when no such commitments existed [⁵]. Hartwell alleges these representations were made knowingly and with intent to induce continued reliance [⁵].

**Count III — Unjust Enrichment (against Meridian):**
Meridian accepted $750,000 from NorthStar Ventures LLC — a prospective investor it was soliciting on Hartwell's behalf — without disclosure or consent, thereby unjustly enriching itself at Hartwell's expense [³] [⁶].
